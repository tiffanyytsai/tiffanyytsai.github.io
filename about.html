about me


My name is Tiffany. I am a recent grad of the York University/Sheridan College Joint Program in Design (YSDN). I am currently located in Vancouver and working in UI/UX. I specialize in interaction, user experience, and identity design.


My approach is always to provoke thought and to question the immeasurables - like novelty and wonder. I am interested in the overlap between novelty and efficiency: how organic emotions and meanings can emerge in a rational and calculated space.


I also love photography, illustration, intimate conversations, gardenia candles, cozy socks, feline friends, towels out of the dryer, amaryllis flowers, the smell of burning wood, sushi, and a good debate.
