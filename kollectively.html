Kollectively Brand and Marketing Strategy

visual identity + website design

Kollectively provides brand and marketing strategy for tech focused companies. They've worked with over 100 clients in Fintech, IoT, Blockchain, Digital Health, Utilities, Logistics, SaaS, and more. They wanted their new visual identity to clearly communicate their expertise in complex technology.


Google Assets: A strong brand is cohesive

As a consultant agency, Kollectively delivers a ton of valuble content through reports and slidedecks. Their team is also entirely remotely. To ensure their clients are always getting content that is polished and on-brand, while their entire team can collaborate in an efficient way, we created assets in Google tools, such as Docs and Slides.

Website: Message that hits home

The website fully showcases the sharp and punchy content Kollectively wanted to drive home. They craft sharp and accurate messaging that needed to be supported and guided by visuals that would not over power.
