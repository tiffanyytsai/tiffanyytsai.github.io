Kollectively Brand and Marketing Strategy

logo + visual identity + website design


Based in Vancouver, Kollectively is a brand and marketing agency that specializes in tech. With over 100 clients in industries ranging from Fintech, IoT, Blockchain, Digital Health, Utilities, Logistics, SaaS, and more, they are 100% focused on technology and innovation. Being the experts in brand positioning and messaging, we worked together to create new visual identity to clearly communicate their expertise in tech.


Google Assets: A strong brand is cohesive

As a consultant agency, Kollectively delivers a ton of valuble content through reports and slidedecks. Their team is also entirely remotely. To ensure their clients are always getting content that is polished and on-brand, while their entire team can collaborate in an efficient way, we created assets in Google tools, such as Docs and Slides.

Website: Message that hits home

The website fully showcases the sharp content Kollectively wanted to drive home. The articulate messaging needed to be supported and guided by visuals that would not over power the copy.
