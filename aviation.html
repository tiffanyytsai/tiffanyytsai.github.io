65 Years of Aviation Accidents

storytelling with data visualization

This project is a visualization of the aviation safety database for five major airlines - Air France, United Airlines, Soviet Air Force, EgyptAir, and Garuda Indonesia - from the years of 1945 to 2010. It focuses on percent fatality of each incident, and illustrates how air travel has become both safer and larger in occupancy.
