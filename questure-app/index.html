QUESTURE



Question + Adventure

Questure is an experimental project I created in response to social media. It focuses on the creation of a free-thinking, whimsical, non-pressuring environment of asking and answering questions.

CONCEPT



Questure was created to explore casual and whimsical spaces, where strangers could freely think-out-loud, converse, and socialize with one another in a liberating and relaxing manner. I like to think of it as new age social media meets old age chatroom game.

KEY DECISIONS



  • Full anonymity

    Questure is fully anoynmous to negate the pressure to answer in any certain way in fear of judgement. Fluid identity aims to induce more discussion and novelty.

  • No down-voting

    Questure relies on a system of positive only reactions to categourize trending discussions and threads.

  • Secondary Feeds

    The stack of questions and asks are the main homepage of Questure. Making the feeds secondary transform the app from passive to active.

REACTIONS

Questure discussions are upvoted and sorted through positive reactions only.

How It Works



USER GENERATED QUESTIONS


Skip through decks of user generated questions. Users cannot go back once questions have been passed to incentivise activity.




CREATIVE ANSWERING


Multiple ways of answering whimsical questions eliminate the fear of answering incorrectly. It strives to get users to create more personal, unique content rather than sharing viral content.




SORT BY FEELING


Reaction based upvoting makes a more intimate, light-hearted atmosphere. Trending screens can be filtered through these, and also further discussion is made on a single thread - a set of question and answer.
