Canmap Systems

Impact Visual Rebrand

In my role as the only designer at Canmap Systems, a software company making specialized utility operations software, I balanced a position between development and marketing. As part of an overhauling rebrand and remessaging process, it was imperative for me to bring clarity into our branding. I decided to priortize within the visual strategy the ideas of technical, straight-forward, and approachable.



Inspired by the idea of foundational blueprints and the assembly forms of utilities, I created multiple illustrations that show simplified processes of a utility's workflow. I also introduced the use of more candid photo imagery of utilities in conjunction to the more technical illustrations. The goal was to be impactful and clear on our positioning, as well as modernizing the existing brand.
