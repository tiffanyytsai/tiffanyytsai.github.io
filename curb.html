Curb App

for better integration between the city and its new residents for garbage collection

try prototype

Curb is a mobile app design created for managing and better informing people about ongoing waste collection and regulations. It is a group project created with Olivier Lord and Danielle McLean. The problem proposed was the amount of unchanging, printed material handed out every year through the government that fixed a year's schedule, as well as fixed recycling information people had that were often outdated. These issues, both found through interviews and personal experience, drove us to develop an application that is fun, vibrant, and easy-to-use.


With Curb, we utilized bold and playful colours to battle stigmatism against the chores of waste disposal. We created vibrant and friendly, but still clean, visuals to reach a wider range of demographics. Go ahead and try our prototype below if you're on a desktop!
