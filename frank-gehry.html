Frank Gehry

Architect Profile Zine

see pdf

This booklet highlights three pieces of architecture by Canadian-American architect, Frank Gehry. The booklet finishes with a work that is still in process, and inspects the process in which Gehry creates his masterpieces.


The colours, a steel blue-green, along with the tones of warm orange and cadnium yellow, represent Gehry's favourite mediums of metal and cedar wood. The booklet itself involved in depth research into an inspiring Canadian artist, and representing his contributions fittingly. Gehry is reknowned for his sketches, and juxtaposing the freeness of his lines to the beautiful, concrete contours of his finished product was a major concept of the book.
