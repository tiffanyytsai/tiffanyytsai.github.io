< filtering by reaction

Simple process of how the filter on trending screen works. Upon tapping out one of the categories, anything from that disappears, and a loading icon is shown at the bottom before more threads appear.






< adding reaction and comment on single thread

When inside a single thread from the "trending screen," here is the process of adding a reaction and also a comment. The thread (a combination of a question and an answer) is fixed, so it is always the top section of the screen. All animations have a bit of an ease-in or ease-out back (inbetween a ease-in and a true bounce), which I think gives it a little bit of playfulness. The reaction becomes faded to give feedback that the action has been performed, and the number slots down at a 100ms delay.






< adding reaction on multithread (trending screen)

This is how to add a reaction without even entering the single thread. It is a faster and more instant process done by holding down on the thread. Icons will appear, and the same sort of UI animation is performed as the single thread animation.






< answering question by drawing

The process of answering a question by the draw option. The reason why there are tabs for either "text" or "draw" is because answers that are submitted by pure text or a canvas take different forms. Even if you wrote on your "drawing canvas" it would export the answer as a screenshot of that canvas, and not pure text. There needs to be a clear seperation of the two forms.
